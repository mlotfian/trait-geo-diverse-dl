Description

Human appropriation of net primary productivity, by terrestrial ecoregion. We derived the map showing the amount of carbon fixed by photosynthesis and then consumed by humans from Imhoff et al. (2004a). A zonal sum procedure was applied to the gridded version of those data to summarize by ecoregion. These data were derived by The Nature Conservancy, and were displayed in a map published in The Atlas of Global Conservation (Hoekstra et al., University of California Press, 2010). More information at http://nature.org/atlas. Data derived from: Imhoff, M. L., L. Bounoua, T. Ricketts, C. Loucks, R. Harriss, and W. T. Lawrence. 2004a. Human Appropriation of Net Primary Productivity (HANPP). Data distributed by the Socioeconomic Data and Applications Center (SEDAC). Available at http://sedac.ciesin.columbia.edu/es/hanpp.html.
